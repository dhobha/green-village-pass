Tourist Discovery: Unveiling the Heart of Our Villages

Welcome to the Green Village Pass, your gateway to authentic rural experiences. Our "Tourist Discovery" program is designed to connect you with the hidden gems and unique stories of our communities. Unlike mass tourism, we focus on providing intimate, sustainable encounters that benefit both travelers and the people who call these places home.
