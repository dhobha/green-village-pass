Earn Rewards for Being Green

Our rewards system is designed to celebrate and encourage your commitment to sustainable tourism. Earn points by completing eco-challenges and engaging with local communities.

Earn Points Easily

Every sustainable action you take, from participating in a waste cleanup to choosing a local homestay, earns you points. Your positive impact is rewarded.

Redeem Exciting Prizes

Use your accumulated points to redeem discounts on future bookings, local artisan crafts, or special village experiences.

See Your Impact Grow

Monitor your points and see the collective difference you and other travelers are making. Our dashboard shows how your actions contribute to a greener future.
